Terms and Conditions

We appreciate your commitment to fitness and your interest in reserving a slot at the gym facility. To ensure a fair distribution of available slots and to provide equal opportunities to all members, we kindly request your cooperation in adhering to the following guidelines:

  1. Eligibility: Regular employee and contractor working for MPI.
  2. Booking Limitations:
    • Please refrain from making reservations for one consecutive day use the gym within a week.
    • If consecutive reservations are detected, our staff may not accept the booking request.
    • Booking opens on Monday and closes on Friday at 11 AM for the upcoming week.
    • If you miss your slot, you can book the next day.
    • Abuse of the system may result in disciplinary action.
  3. Daily Slot Availability: Please be aware that our gym has a maximum of 5 slots available per day. Your understanding and cooperation in adhering to this limit will help us accommodate as many members as possible.

Remember: First Register, First Served! Slots are assigned on a first-come, first-served basis. Ensure to register promptly when booking opens to secure your preferred time.

We appreciate your cooperation in maintaining a fair and inclusive gym environment. If you have any questions or concerns, feel free to contact our gym staff.

Thank you!
